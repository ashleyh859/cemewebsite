Past Events 活动

2024 Spring: CEME Annual Concert

At the end of the academic year, CEME is proud to showcase all our hard work. Our large ensemble, consisting of traditional Chinese and modern Western instruments, perform many traditional Chinese orchestral pieces. In addition, our members show off their talents in solo an dsmall ensemble pieces. Our repertoire includes traditional Chinese music, Asian pop music, film soundtracks, and even East-West fusion.


April 14, 2024 View Our Entire Performance Here

2023 Fall: Asian Music Night 亚洲音乐之夜

Hosted by CEME, Asian Music Night is a collaboration of Asian music and performance groups on campus. From song, dance, to martial arts, performances showcasing different cultures and traditions across Asia are presented to the Cornell community. East Asian snacks, food, and drinks are provided.
