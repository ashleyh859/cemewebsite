Our Audition Process

Fall: Asian Music Night

Hosted by CEME, Asian Music Night is a collaboration of Asian music and performance groups on campus. From song, dance, to martial arts, performances showcasing different cultures and traditions across Asia are presented to the Cornell community. East Asian snacks, food, and drinks are provided.

Spring: CEME Annual Concert

At the end of the academic year, CEME is proud to showcase all our hard work. Our large ensemble, consisting of traditional Chinese and modern Western instruments, perform many traditional Chinese orchestral pieces. In addition, our members show off their talents in solo and small ensemble pieces. Our repertoire includes traditional Chinese music, Asian pop music, film soundtracks, and even East-West fusion.

Instrument Workshops

Each year, CEME organizes Instrument Workshops, offering beginners an opportunity to learn how to play an instrument, delve into its rich history and culture, and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Guided by experienced members, the workshops take place on Saturday mornings at Lincoln Hall, featuring two 1.5-hour sessions. Participants of all levels are welcome, with the sessions tailored to individual experiences.
